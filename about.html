About Amani Construction

Zimbabwe's #1 Construction & Renovation Specialists

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Amani Construction has grown to become Zimbabwe's most trusted name in construction and renovation. What started as a small team with a passion for quality craftsmanship has evolved into Harare's leading construction company, serving over 127 satisfied clients.

Our name "Amani" means "peace" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to providing peace of mind through exceptional workmanship and reliable service. Every project we undertake is backed by our industry-leading 5-year warranty and our promise of on-time delivery.

From kitchen renovations to waterproofing, tiling to painting, we handle every aspect of your construction needs with professionalism and expertise that sets us apart in the industry.
